## **DAO Tokens**

- Each DAO has a supply of 1 billion DAO tokens.
  - During the raise, 850 million DAO tokens are for sale.
  - The remaining 150 million DAO tokens are reserved to seed the liquidity pool.

## **Raised SOL**

- 80% of the raised SOL goes to seed the liquidity pool.
- 20% of the raised SOL goes to the AUM.

## **Fees**

- Daos Fun pools have a 1% fee.
  - 0.5% of all transactions go to the creator.
  - 0.45% of all transactions go to Daos Fun.
  - 0.05% of all transactions go to the referrer (if applicable).
