## vote to lift trading restrictions

- currently, this is the only meaningful vote that can take place on the platform. 
- navigate to the "dao" tab 
- anyone who is a token holder can click "request lift restrictions"
- this will take a snapshot of all current token holders. 
- voting will be open for 3 days.
- there is a quorum of 5% of the token supply.
